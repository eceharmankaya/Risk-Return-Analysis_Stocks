# Risk-Return-Analysis_Stocks
Risk–return analysis of selected technology stocks using Excel, including average return, volatility, and scatter plot visualization.

Tools: Microsoft Excel

# Risk–Return Analysis of Selected Technology Stocks

This project shows the relationship between the average returns of selected technology stocks and the risk values they carry.

## Stocks
AAPL, AMZN, MSFT, NVDA, TSLA

## Method
Daily returns were calculated using historical stock price data. After that, the average return and volatility (risk) values of each stock were computed in Excel. A scatter plot was created to visualize the relationship between risk and return.

## Analysis

- The graph shows the average returns of the stocks and the risk values they carry.

- Looking at the graph, NVDA provides the highest return and it appears at the top.

- Since all of these stocks belong to the technology sector, the popularization of artificial intelligence and the increase in its usage may have affected these stocks both positively and negatively.

- TSLA has a very high level of risk. This can be explained by the fact that prices in technology companies with high growth potential can increase more rapidly.

- AMZN and MSFT belong to larger companies and they have more diversified income sources and more established business structures.

- Overall, the graph shows that higher risk values are associated with higher return values.
